# Data-Mining-Project
This is very importance project that help us to understand data mining methods and algorithms.

The global telecommunications industry is one of the most dynamic and competitive sectors in the modern economy. With deregulation and technological advancements, customers face minimal barriers to switching service providers, resulting in a phenomenon known as customer churn.Therefore, the ability to predict and prevent churn has become a paramount strategic objective for sustaining profitability and long-term viability in this saturated market.
